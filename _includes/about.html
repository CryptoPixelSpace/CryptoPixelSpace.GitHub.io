About Cryptopixelspace

Cryptopixelspace is an open game world based on Ethereum blockchain decentralization and virtual meta universe.

21,000 unique, randomly generated pixel people will enter CryptoPixelSpace!

10% will be allocated to partners and small public draws.

Cryptopixelspace NFT holders can import their NFT into the game.

In the future, cryptopixelspace may support the import of real-world object scanning into the game.

Because the whole platform is based on blockchain technology, cryptopixelspace expects players to control the whole game world.

You can check the rarity introduction about CryptoPixelSpaceNFT on GitHub.
